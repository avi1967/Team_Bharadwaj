Our Mission

We aim to revolutionize aeromodelling by crafting innovative fixed-wing aircraft and multirotor drones, competing at the highest levels while fostering a culture of creativity and technical excellence.

Our Vision

To inspire and lead the next generation of aerospace innovators, building a legacy of knowledge, passion, and groundbreaking achievements in the skies.

Our Approach

We thrive on collaboration, with specialized teams in design, simulation, fabrication, and testing working in sync to transform ideas into high-flying realities.

Our Impact

Through workshops, mentorship, and competitions, we empower students, advance UAV technology, and leave a lasting mark on the aerospace community.
